Kimchi Recipe

Prep: 30 minutes | Ferment: 48 hours

Makes: 1 large jar

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients:

  • 1 napa cabbage
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes)
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • Garlic, ginger, scallions

Instructions:

  1. Cut cabbage into pieces and salt generously.
  2. Let it sit for 2 hours, then rinse.
  3. Mix garlic, ginger, gochugaru, sugar, and fish sauce to create a paste.
  4. Massage the paste into the cabbage.
  5. Pack tightly into a jar and let ferment for 2-3 days.
  6. Store in the fridge and serve as needed.
